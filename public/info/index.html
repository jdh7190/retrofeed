Welcome!

This is a Retro Themed Open Social Client on Bitcoin where users can discuss anything and everything.

Posts, Likes, Tipping and Chat are currently supported.

Posting is almost free. You need a HandCash wallet to interact.

Likes cost 1 cent.

Emoji reactions to chat messages cost 1,000 satoshis.

The platform takes 10% of all interactions.

Click any thumbnail of an unfurled URL to get a clean reader view of the article.

Enjoy!
